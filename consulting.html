Consulting

Aviation Consulting

Anjuna Aviation Consulting understands the world of aviation and has developed a range of services that can deliver seamless results across a number of specializations. We’re able to help airlines and airports with high-level strategic planning, day to day operations, and more.

Our experienced team of consultants work with our airline partners on network planning and route development including choice of routes, aircraft, frequency, scheduling, and pricing. In addition, our consultants help leverage the most appropriate partners and help to identify alliance, joint network and codeshare opportunities.

Everyone knows data is valuable, and most understand all the data in the world is useless if you don’t know how to leverage it. Everything we do at Anjuna is driven by the data, which we leverage together with our local relationships and market knowledge. The core to this is our Anjuna Intelligence tool that generates actionable insights.

Our team of industry experts have held senior management positions in over 20 countries around the world, allowing Anjuna Aviation Consulting to give every client a range of qualified, informed perspectives..
