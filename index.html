About Us

Anjuna partners with airlines and airports around the world to generate incremental revenue, lower distribution costs and improve yield in domestic and international markets.

Anjuna offers all the services of a traditional airline general sales agent (GSA), with an in-house aviation consulting division. However the core of our success is our Anjuna Intelligence™ platform that leverages multiple data sources in real-time to identify incremental opportunities and network planning opportunities that capitalize on new and existing market segments which create operational efficiencies to reduce costs.

Anjuna's 17 worldwide offices provide its' clients a global reach and local expertise through onsite management teams with well established relationships.

Our team

The Anjuna team is comprised of the best in aviation, travel, IT, digital marketing and problem solvers who analyze things differently. Anjuna's cross-functional team thinks together, but not alike, offering its' airline and airport clients a unique outside perspective. Utilizing the latest technology, Anjuna leverages its' global network of subject matter experts in various disciplines and combines this with a regional sales team with the local expertise and relationships to drive incremental revenue, increase yield and reduce costs. This non-silo'd business structure enables Anjuna to scale quickly, efficiently and to provide its' clients with a consistent level of service throughout its' network.

Claire Gray

Executive Chairman

Claire Gray is the Co-founder of Corporate Travel Management (CTM), the largest independent corporate travel agency group in Asia Pacific. During CTMs IPO, she was acting Executive Director to the Board. Claire was also the Co-Founder & Executive Director of Travelogic as well as Eventlogic, and a Global Board Member & Global Operating Committee Representative ASPAC of Globalstar. Claire holds an MBA in Global Management from Thunderbird School of Management, and speaks fluent English and French.

James Vaile

CEO

James Vaile, the Co-Founder and CEO of Anjuna has thirty seven years of global travel industry experience. He has successfully managed twenty airlines across Asia Pacific, heading the largest GSA in the Asia Pacific Region. Prior to this, James was an executive with Expedia holding the positions of Vice President of Europe and then Vice President of Asia Pacific. More recently he was the launch Chairman of the first Asia Pacific Meta Search travel company based in Singapore.

Patrick Stepanek

COO

Patrick Stepanek is Anjuna’s Chief Operating Officer with fifteen years of management experience in the aerospace and digital mass customization industry. Prior to being named Anjuna’s COO, he successfully set up and managed Vistaprint's global, cross-functional research and digital engineering organization. Before this, Patrick worked as a Business Unit Manager in the aerospace sector for CIG. He holds an MBA in Global Management from Thunderbird School of Management, as well as a PhD in Aerospace Engineering in cooperation with Airbus. He speaks fluent English, German, Italian and Czech.
