Cargo

Cargo Handling

Anjuna is a global Airline Cargo GSSA offering local expertise through onsite management teams with well established relationships, that help our partner airlines to generate incremental revenue, lower distribution costs and improve yields in international markets.

With 17 offices and partners worldwide, Anjuna offers tailormade Aviation Cargo GSSA solutions in key markets across the globe, including specialized sales, innovative marketing, efficient operations, personalized customer service and simplified accounting, supported by our business intelligence solutions.

Anjuna’s cross-functional team thinks together, but not alike, offering its’ airline clients a unique perspective and ultimately the ability to generate incremental revenue and increase yield.

Cargo Management

  • Offline and Online cargo office as the airline
  • Helpdesk support for local freight forwarders
  • Installation of the airline’s own Cargo System
  • Coordination with warehouse and first carrier (for off-line)
  • Help implement Special Pro-rate Agreements (SPA) and interline network
  • Global Cargo Interline Platform (GP-275 partnership)

Sales, Analysis and Support

  • Full Cargo reporting (CSR) plus monthly reconciliation
  • Processing of invoices/local taxation as required
  • Handling of customer issues and claims
  • Track-and-Trace
  • Display and distribution of promotional literature
  • Rate analysis
  • Sales & Marketing

Jon Murray

Business Development & Cargo Manager

Jon Murray, a native of Southern Ontario, has over 25 years of experience in the travel industry having worked for Flight Centre and Korean Air before joining Anjuna. Jon has successfully lead the launch of both Oman Air and Fiji Airways in the Canadian market and holds the IATA cargo diploma including Cargo Supply Chain, Transport Modes and Dangerous Goods Certifications.

Barneet Kohli

Airline Cargo Sales Manager

Barneet Kohli, Anjuna's Airline Cargo Manager has over 15 years of Airline Cargo industry experience. Before joining Anjuna, Barneet worked for Sharaf Cargo LLC, an Airline Cargo GSA, where he started out as a managment trainee working his way up to Assistant General Manager during his 13-year tenure. During this time he represented 26 airlines from across the globe including Delta Cargo, South African Airways, Finnair, Sri Lankan Airlines and Gulf Air. Barneet holds a post graduate degree in management, finance and speaks fluent English, Arabic and Hindi.
